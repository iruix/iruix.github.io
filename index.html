Hello, my name is Braian Jullinen. Since this site is about me, you're out of luck. I'll write about myself as long as I want to. I've been fascinated by IT from a young age as I built my first computer with the help of my father when I was 7. I have always been interested in tech, but nearing the end of high school I started working more with computers.

First IT job was being IT support to all of my family. In high school a good friend of mine got me more into programming so I played around with different languages and decided to go learn IT in Tallinn University. My university journey got cut short, because I found a job as a Wordpress Web Developer, that was exactly what I needed at that time. I have been working as a Web Developer now for around 2 years and have been passionate about technology for 16+ years.

I've done most of my development in WordPress. Mainly we use page builders in my work but I've used lots of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SCSS etc. My work mainly is doing solo projects, but in my short time at another company, we worked in a team and I loved it.
